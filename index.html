Biography

I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Sichuan University, under the supervision of Prof. Yanan Sun. I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from the School of Xihua University in 2018 and 2021, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Peng. Additionally, from July to September 2023, I visited Assoc. Prof. Chao Qian's research group at Nanjing University.

Research Interests

I am interested in evolutionary computation, neural networks, and theoretical analysis of evolutionary algorithms.
